PolygonGraphics

PolygonGraphics is a polygon visualization for the entity.

  • Cesium element: PolygonGraphics
  • Example: PolygonGraphics

Availability​

PolygonGraphics can be mounted only insideEntity components, and can not be mounted more than once for each entity.
